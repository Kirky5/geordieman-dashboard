  • Consider a sprint tri: Racing a shorter event in July gives invaluable race experience
  • Weekly volume: ~10-12 hours

Phase 6: Taper & Race Week

🚨 Open Water Swimming

Pool swimming and open water are very different. Sighting, drafting, cold water, no walls. Start OW sessions as soon as possible — May at the latest. Consider a guided clinic. Wetsuit helps with buoyancy and warmth. This is your #1 gap to close in the next 3 months.

⚠️ Brick Sessions (Bike→Run)

Running off the bike feels completely different — legs like concrete for the first 1-2km. You must practice this. Start short (45min bike → 15min run) and build. By July you should be doing 90min bike → 40min run bricks fortnightly. Your Hyrox experience with heavy-leg running will help.

⚠️ Race-Day Nutrition

5+ hours burns ~4,000-5,000 calories. Plan: light breakfast 3h before, gel at swim exit, 60-90g carbs/hour on bike (gels/bars/electrolytes), gels/cola on run. Never try anything new on race day. Test everything in training first — start experimenting in Phase 2.
