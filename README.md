## What's Tracked

- **Overview** — sessions, calories, HR zones, weekly volume (recent Garmin data)
- **History** — 6 years of Strava data (Apr 2020–Mar 2026): annual running volume, monthly charts, key milestones, discipline backgrounds
- **VO₂ Max** — trend, classification gauge, path to Superior (54→55-56+)
- **Running** — pace trends, cadence, stride analysis, all-time top 10 longest runs
- **Cycling** — Zwift power data, NP trends, outdoor cycling history
- **Sleep** — 12 months of sleep score, duration, bedtime patterns
- **Race Predictor** — Garmin predicted race times (5K, 10K, HM, Marathon)
- **Geordieman 70.3** — dual race targets (Sub-6h primary / Sub-5:30 stretch), discipline readiness, 24-week training plan, live countdown
- **Insights** — key observations and priority recommendations
